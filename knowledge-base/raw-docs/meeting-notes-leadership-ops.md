## Discussion: Internal Tooling Gaps
Founders raised frustration that preparing for this monthly review currently requires each department head to manually pull numbers from five different systems (Salesforce, Harvest, the recruiting spreadsheet, the CS feedback sheet, and Finance's internal reports) with no single source of truth.

**Decision:** Approved a small internal project to build:
1. A centralized, AI-searchable knowledge base for company documentation (reducing onboarding/support friction)
2. An automated triage workflow for incoming customer feedback (routing negative feedback to Customer Success immediately instead of waiting for scheduled check-ins)
3. A lightweight executive dashboard pulling key metrics from existing tools, so this monthly review doesn't require manual data pulls
