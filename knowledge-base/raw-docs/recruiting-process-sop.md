# Recruiting Process SOP

**Department:** Recruiting
**Last Updated:** February 2026
**Owner:** Head of Recruiting

## Candidate Pipeline Stages
1. **Applied**
2. **Resume Screened**
3. **Phone Screen** (30 min, Recruiting-led)
4. **Technical/Case Interview** (60 min, Delivery-led)
5. **Final Interview** (with Head of Department)
6. **Offer Extended**
7. **Onboarded**

Currently tracked in a shared spreadsheet (`Recruiting Pipeline 2026`), updated manually by whoever conducts each stage. **Known issue:** this frequently falls out of sync when multiple people update it simultaneously — flagged as a candidate for automation.

## Contractor Pool
Vantage Point maintains a bench of 15–20 vetted contractors for engagements that exceed current full-time capacity. Contractors go through an abbreviated 2-stage process (Phone Screen + Technical Interview) and are re-vetted every 12 months.

## Standard Interview Questions by Role

**Consultant/Engineer roles:**
- Walk through a project where you had to translate a business problem into a technical solution
- How would you approach a client who wants AI adoption but has messy/undocumented data?

**Sales/Customer Success roles:**
- Describe how you've handled a client relationship at risk of churning
- Walk through your discovery process for a new prospect

## Offer Process
- Offers require Head of Department + Finance sign-off (for compensation banding)
- Standard offer window: 5 business days
- Background checks are run through Checkr post-offer-acceptance, before start date is confirmed

## Time-to-Fill Target
Company goal is 30 days from job posting to offer acceptance for full-time roles; currently averaging 41 days as of Q1 2026.
