# Service Offerings FAQ

**Department:** Product/Delivery
**Last Updated:** March 2026
**Owner:** Head of Delivery

## What services does Vantage Point offer?

**1. AI Readiness Assessment** (2–3 weeks)
A diagnostic engagement evaluating a client's data infrastructure, workflows, and team readiness for AI adoption. Deliverable: a prioritized roadmap document.

**2. AI Implementation Engagement** (8–12 weeks)
Full build-out of a specific AI/automation use case — e.g., a customer support triage system, an internal knowledge base, or a reporting dashboard. Includes a 2-week hypercare period post-launch.

**3. AI Ops Advisory (Retainer)**
Ongoing monthly retainer (20 or 40 hours/month) for clients who want continued support after an implementation — monitoring, iteration, and ad hoc automation requests.

## What industries do we typically serve?
Retail/e-commerce, healthcare administration (non-clinical), and logistics/supply chain. We do not currently take on clinical healthcare, financial services requiring SOC 2 Type II, or government contracts.

## What's our typical team structure on an engagement?
- 1 Engagement Lead (client-facing, manages scope/timeline)
- 1–2 Consultants/Engineers (build the solution)
- Fractional support from a Delivery Director for QA and escalations

## What tools do we typically build with?
Claude API, OpenAI API, n8n, Zapier, Python, SQL, and standard BI tools (Looker, Metabase, or client-preferred). Tool choice depends on the client's existing stack — we avoid recommending vendor lock-in when a client already has infrastructure in place.

## How do we handle scope changes mid-engagement?
Any scope change beyond the original SOW requires a Change Order, approved by the Engagement Lead and communicated to the client with updated pricing/timeline before work begins.
