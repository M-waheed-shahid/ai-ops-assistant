# Employee Onboarding Guide

**Department:** Operations
**Last Updated:** March 2026
**Owner:** People Ops Team

## Welcome to Vantage Point Consulting

This guide covers everything you need for your first two weeks.

## Week 1: Setup

- **Day 1:** IT will provision your laptop, email (@vantagepointconsulting.com), Slack, and Google Workspace access. Your manager will schedule a welcome call.
- **Day 1–2:** Complete required trainings in the LMS: Security & Data Handling, Anti-Harassment, and Client Confidentiality.
- **Day 2–3:** Set up your 1:1 cadence with your manager (default is weekly, 30 minutes).
- **Day 3–5:** Shadow a live client call if one is scheduled for your team; otherwise review the most recent client deliverable in your practice area.

## Week 2: Ramp-Up

- Meet with a "buddy" (assigned by your manager) for informal Q&A.
- Complete department-specific onboarding checklist (see your department's folder in Drive).
- Attend the Friday all-hands to be introduced to the company.

## Accounts & Tools You'll Need
- Google Workspace (email, calendar, drive)
- Slack
- Notion (internal wiki)
- Harvest (time tracking — required for all billable staff)
- Salesforce (Sales & Customer Success only)

## Who to Contact
- IT issues: it-support@vantagepointconsulting.com
- Payroll/benefits: finance@vantagepointconsulting.com
- General HR questions: people-ops@vantagepointconsulting.com

## Probation & Review
All new hires have a 90-day check-in with their manager and a People Ops representative to discuss ramp progress and answer any open questions.
