# Sales Process SOP

**Department:** Sales
**Last Updated:** February 2026
**Owner:** Head of Sales

## Pipeline Stages (in Salesforce)
1. **Lead** — inbound or outbound contact identified, not yet qualified
2. **Qualified** — BANT criteria confirmed (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline)
3. **Discovery Call Scheduled**
4. **Proposal Sent**
5. **Negotiation**
6. **Closed Won / Closed Lost**

Every lead must be logged in Salesforce within 24 hours of first contact. Deals sitting in one stage for more than 21 days without an update trigger an automatic Slack reminder to the deal owner and their manager.

## Discovery Call Checklist
- Confirm the client's current tooling/tech stack
- Identify the specific business problem and desired outcome
- Confirm decision-making process and timeline
- Identify budget range (do not skip this — proposals without a budget range take 3x longer to close)

## Proposal Process
1. Use the standard proposal template (Notion: Sales > Templates > Proposal Template v4)
2. Draft pricing using the Rate Card (updated quarterly by Finance)
3. Proposals over $75,000 require Head of Sales sign-off before sending
4. Proposals must be sent within 5 business days of the discovery call

## Contract & Handoff
- Once a deal is Closed Won, Sales schedules a kickoff call with the assigned Delivery lead within 5 business days
- A completed Client Intake Form (Notion) must be filled out before kickoff
- Sales remains the primary point of contact for the client through the first 2 weeks of delivery, then transitions fully to the assigned Delivery lead

## Reporting
Weekly pipeline review every Monday at 10am. Each rep reports: new leads, stalled deals, and forecast for the month.
