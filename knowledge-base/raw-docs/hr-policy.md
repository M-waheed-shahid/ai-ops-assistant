# HR Policy Handbook (Summary)

**Department:** Operations / People Ops
**Last Updated:** January 2026
**Owner:** People Ops Team

## Paid Time Off (PTO)
- Full-time employees accrue 18 PTO days per year, front-loaded on January 1st for employees who start before March 1st; prorated otherwise.
- PTO requests must be submitted in the HR portal at least 5 business days in advance, except for illness.
- Unused PTO: up to 5 days may roll over into the next calendar year. Anything above that is forfeited unless required otherwise by state law.

## Remote Work Policy
- Vantage Point operates as a hybrid company. Employees within commuting distance of the Austin office are expected in-office Tuesday and Thursday.
- Fully remote roles are permitted with VP-level approval and are noted in the offer letter.
- Remote employees must maintain reliable internet (min. 25 Mbps) and a private workspace for client calls.

## Sick Leave
- Sick leave is separate from PTO: 8 days per year, does not roll over.
- Employees should notify their manager and post in the #out-sick Slack channel before 9:30am local time.

## Holidays
Vantage Point observes 10 paid company holidays annually, published each December for the following year in the HR portal.

## Expense Reimbursement
- Business expenses under $75 can be self-approved and submitted via Ramp.
- Expenses over $75 require manager pre-approval.
- All receipts must be submitted within 30 days of the expense.

## Performance Reviews
- Formal reviews occur twice a year (June and December).
- Compensation adjustments, if any, are communicated within 2 weeks of the December review cycle.

## Questions
Direct any policy questions to people-ops@vantagepointconsulting.com. This document is a summary; the full handbook is in the HR portal under "Policies."
