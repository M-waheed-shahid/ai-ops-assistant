# Finance & Invoicing Policy

**Department:** Finance
**Last Updated:** January 2026
**Owner:** Head of Finance

## Client Invoicing
- Fixed-fee engagements: invoiced in 3 installments — 40% at kickoff, 40% at midpoint milestone, 20% at final delivery
- Retainer engagements: invoiced monthly, in advance, on the 1st of each month
- Payment terms: Net 30 for all clients unless otherwise negotiated

## Time Tracking Requirement
All billable staff must log hours in Harvest daily. Time entries older than 5 days without submission trigger an automated reminder email. Unlogged time cannot be included in client invoices, which directly impacts revenue recognition — this is treated as a compliance issue, not just an admin task.

## Project Budgeting
- Every engagement gets a budget in the Finance system tied to its Salesforce opportunity
- Engagement Leads receive a budget utilization alert at 75% and 90% of allocated hours
- Overages beyond 100% require a written explanation from the Engagement Lead in the monthly finance review

## Payroll
- Payroll runs semi-monthly (15th and last business day of the month)
- Direct deposit is required for all employees; set up via Gusto within your first 3 days

## Expense Policy
See HR Policy document for reimbursement thresholds. All expenses must include: date, amount, business purpose, and client/project code if billable to a client.

## Vendor Payments
Vendor invoices are processed net-30 through Bill.com. New vendors require a signed W-9 and an approved Vendor Intake Form before payment can be issued.
