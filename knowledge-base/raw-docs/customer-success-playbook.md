# Customer Success Playbook

**Department:** Customer Success
**Last Updated:** March 2026
**Owner:** Head of Customer Success

## Post-Engagement Handoff
When a Delivery engagement ends, Customer Success receives a handoff packet from the Engagement Lead containing: final deliverables, known limitations, and a client health assessment (Green/Yellow/Red).

## Client Health Check-Ins
- **Retainer clients:** monthly check-in call
- **Completed project clients (no retainer):** check-in at 30, 60, and 90 days post-delivery to assess adoption and gauge renewal/upsell interest

## Feedback Collection
- A post-engagement survey (Google Form) is sent to the primary client contact within 1 week of project completion
- Survey covers: satisfaction (1–5 scale), likelihood to recommend (NPS), and open-text feedback
- **Known gap:** survey responses currently land in a Google Sheet with no automatic categorization or alerting — negative feedback can go unnoticed until the next scheduled check-in. This is a top candidate for automation.

## Escalation Process
If a client health score drops to "Red" (via check-in or survey), Customer Success must:
1. Notify the Head of Customer Success and original Engagement Lead within 24 hours
2. Schedule a call with the client within 3 business days
3. Log the issue and resolution plan in the Client Health Tracker (Notion)

## Renewal Process (Retainer Clients)
- Renewal conversations start 60 days before contract end date
- Pricing for renewals is reviewed by Finance; increases are capped at CPI + 3% unless scope has materially changed

## Churn Review
Any client who does not renew is reviewed in the monthly Customer Success sync to identify patterns (pricing, service gaps, staffing changes, etc.).
