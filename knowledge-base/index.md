## Known Gaps Referenced Across Documents

- Scattered, hard-to-search internal documentation (Onboarding Guide, HR Policy, multiple SOPs)
- No automatic triage/alerting for negative customer feedback (Customer Success Playbook)
- No single source of truth for leadership reporting — manual data pulls from 5 systems (Leadership & Ops Meeting Notes)
- Recruiting pipeline spreadsheet frequently falls out of sync (Recruiting Process SOP)
