# Company Profile: Vantage Point Consulting

## Overview
Vantage Point Consulting is a 34-person boutique technology consulting firm based in Austin, TX. Founded in 2021, the company helps mid-sized businesses ($10M–$150M revenue) adopt AI, automation, and data infrastructure. Clients span retail, healthcare admin, and logistics. The firm operates in project-based engagements (avg. 8–12 weeks) alongside a smaller retainer-based "AI Ops" advisory line.

## Departments
- **Sales** (5 people) — outbound + inbound deal pipeline, client proposals, contract negotiation
- **Recruiting** (2 people) — hiring consultants and engineers, managing contractor pool
- **Product/Delivery** (12 people) — consultants and engineers executing client projects
- **Customer Success** (3 people) — post-engagement support, renewals, satisfaction tracking
- **Marketing** (3 people) — content, webinars, case studies, lead generation
- **Operations** (4 people) — internal tooling, vendor management, scheduling, HR admin
- **Finance** (3 people) — invoicing, project budgeting, payroll, expense tracking

## Company Pain Points

1. **Scattered documentation.** Onboarding guides, SOPs, and client playbooks are spread across Google Drive, Notion, and email threads. New hires and contractors waste days hunting for basic answers ("What's our PTO policy?" "What's the standard proposal template?").

2. **No visibility into customer feedback.** Post-project survey responses and support emails are logged inconsistently (some in a spreadsheet, some nowhere). Negative feedback about a project sometimes doesn't reach leadership until a client is already unhappy about renewal.

3. **Manual reporting for leadership.** The founders currently ask each department head to send a Friday status email. There's no single dashboard showing pipeline health, delivery capacity, recruiting funnel, or finance metrics — everything is reconstructed manually before board meetings.

4. **Recruiting bottlenecks.** With a growing contractor pool, tracking candidate status (screened, interviewed, offered, onboarded) happens in a shared spreadsheet that frequently goes out of sync.

5. **Repetitive manual triage.** Inbound leads, support tickets, and feedback all require a human to read, categorize, and route them — a low-value task that eats hours weekly across Sales, Customer Success, and Ops.

## Why This Matters for the AI Ops Assistant Project
Vantage Point is small enough that no department has dedicated automation or BI headcount, but complex enough that the pain is real — making it a realistic target for the three deliverables in this project:
- A **knowledge base** that solves pain point #1
- An **automation workflow** that solves pain point #5 (and feeds data into #2)
- An **executive dashboard** that solves pain point #3
